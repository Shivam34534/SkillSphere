# 🚀 SkillSphere: Simplified Project Guide

### What is SkillSphere?
Imagine **Fiverr** and **LinkedIn** had a baby specifically for your college campus. 

It is a private website where only students from your college can join. It’s a place where you can hire your friends to help with projects (like making a logo or coding a website) or get paid to do those things yourself.

---

### Why did we build this?
1.  **Trust:** No strangers! Everyone is a verified student with a college email.
2.  **Safety:** We use a "Safe-Box" (Escrow) system. Your money is only given to the worker once you are happy with the work.
3.  **Real Experience:** You get to build a real portfolio and earn money while you are still studying.

---

### How does it work? (The 1-2-3 Step)
1.  **Post:** You post a job (e.g., "I need a 10-slide PPT for Biology").
2.  **Work:** Someone applies, you chat with them, and they start working.
3.  **Pay:** Once they finish and you like the work, the "Safe-Box" opens and they get paid in their digital wallet.

---

### Key Features
*   **Role Selection:** You can be a **Student** (hiring), a **Freelancer** (working), or a **Club** (recruiting).
*   **Digital Wallet:** Every user has a wallet to track their earnings.
*   **Chat:** Built-in messaging to talk about the work.
*   **XP & Levels:** The more you work, the more "XP" you gain to show everyone you are a pro.

---

### The Tech Behind It (In simple words)
*   **Frontend (The Face):** Made with **React** to look beautiful and modern.
*   **Backend (The Brain):** Powered by **Node.js** to handle all the logic and security.
*   **Database (The Memory):** Uses **MongoDB** to remember all the users and jobs.
*   **Real-time:** Uses **Socket.io** so your messages send instantly, just like WhatsApp.
